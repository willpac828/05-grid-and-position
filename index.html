Week 1

HTML & CSS

I didn't know anything about layouts or placement, really, before the beginning of last week. I had done tutorials and was familiar with a handful of CSS properties, but designing layouts is a whole 'nutha animal.

I'm more comfortable today with the difference between styling types such as inline-block and floats than I was a couple of days ago. I still find that I am having to guess and check on almost every instance of styling, but I'm confident that it will get better.
